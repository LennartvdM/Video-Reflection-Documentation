# 5.2 Footage Organization

After gathering debriefing insights, the next step is structuring the footage organization. Each recorded piece is assigned a distinctive identifier, incorporating the surgical case, date, and pertinent specifics into its filename. This meticulous categorization ensures future access is effortless. This streamlined approach reduces ambiguity, ensuring the valuable content remains easily accessible.
